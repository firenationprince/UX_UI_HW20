Project Brief

THE PROBLEM: The "Taking it to the Streets" web page is not updated, it is not easy to see and understand at first glance what the page is about. It is messy, many buttons lead to the same thing and each page does not match the other in terms of design.

THE SOLUTION: Redesign the "Taking to the Streets" web page so that potential volunteers register in a faster and easier way, create a page where the objective of this non-profit organization is understood.

The Challenge

The short timeline was definitely a big constraint in the project. I was out of the country for almost all of it and had little access to wifi over there as well as getting sick towards the end of the project which was another constraint. The unique factors in the project was the real face lifting of this website and giving it a redesign without losing its prime factors of donating and volunteering as a focus.

Goals

The goals were to create an app to help chronic ill people or supporters be able to connect with others during treatments or hospital appointments. The goals are as follow:

  • Increasing public awareness of the problem of homelessness and the work that taking it to The Streets is doing.
  • Describing the services offered by the organization.
  • Facilitating communication and collaboration between the organization, volunteers, donors, and people in general.
  • Enhancing the organization’s credibility and trustworthiness.

User Persona

Problem Statement

The website we produce helps Taking it to the Streets accomplish ways for their local community to get involve and donate by providing intuitive and easy to navigate content that makes potential volunteers feel encourage to participate within the nonprofit. So they can be part an active participant of their community work.

Value Proposition

The organization Taking it to the street is developing ways for their local community to get involve and donate in order to help the people who are homeless in San Antonio with weekly services.
We’re better because we found more effective ways to increase community engagement via donations and volunteering.
We’re believable because we focus on making sure new volunteers get involved via community engament, potential partners and volunterring.
